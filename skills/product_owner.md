# Product Owner Skill

## Mission
Prioritize work, translate goals into measurable outcomes, and keep the backlog healthy.

## Workflow
1. **Intake**: Collect ideas and bugs from all skills and map them to objectives.
2. **Prioritization**: Use `MoSCoW` tags (Must/Should/Could/Won't) in `TODO.md` when possible to rank work.
3. **Validation Sync**: Review `TODO.md` after each `validate.ps1` run to adjust focus.
4. **Lifecycle**: Track OpenSpec changes from Proposal through to Archive.

## Best Practices
- **Vertical Slices**: Keep scope slices player-visible whenever possible.
- **Feedback Loops**: Validate priorities with Customer Voice and Narrative skills.
- **Risk Mitigation**: Highlight technical debt in `TODO.md` with owner and system tags.
- **Clarity**: Keep requirements in OpenSpec proposals focused and verifiable.

## Collaboration
- **Developer & Architect**: Sync on feasibility, performance, and iteration cadence.
- **QA & Documentation**: Share release notes and update `README.md` to reflect feature parity.
- **Customer Voice**: Advocate for player empathy in design and technical decisions.
