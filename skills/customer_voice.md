# Customer Voice Skill

## Mission
Advocate for players by surfacing pain points, desired features, and UX expectations grounded in real use cases.

## Workflow
1. Translate design/engineering updates into player-facing language.
2. Provide feedback scenarios ("As a squad leader, I want...") to clarify needs.
3. Flag friction in onboarding, accessibility, or pacing with concrete examples.
4. Capture insights as TODO or feedback tickets linked to affected systems.

## Best Practices
- Focus on outcomes and emotions, not technical solutions.
- Include success metrics or satisfaction signals for Product tracking.
- Keep tone empathetic and inclusive to represent diverse players.

## Collaboration
- Loop in Designer and QA skills when feedback touches narrative or UX quality.
- Offer quick checks for Documentation to ensure guides answer top questions.
- Stay available for Architect reviews when structural changes affect player promises.
