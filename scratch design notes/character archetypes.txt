Grit	Stone / earth	Endurance, persistence, reliability; withstands attrition	Neutral matter; resists environmental stress (flood, mud, heat)
Flow	River / liquid	Adaptability, responsiveness, flexibility; goes with the current	Neutral matter; adjusts to changing terrain/weather; supports allies
Gusto	Wind / gust	Momentum, initiative, assertive push; directional energy	Neutral energy; moves events, characters, and weather trends; not warm
Clarity	Clear sky	Insight, perception, foresight; reads situations accurately	Neutral perception; balances extremes, prevents runaway effects of warm/cold axes
Temper	Cold front / ice	Restraint, discipline, suppression; controlled intensity	Cold energy; dampens effects, slows momentum, reduces volatility
Shine	Sunbeam / ambient glow	Gentle influence, inspiration, sustained warmth; morale, encouragement	Warm energy; soft amplification, persistent buffs, morale or subtle environmental effect
